Team8Paper

Team8Paper is an intuitive note taking web-app that allows you to create notes anywhere on the screen, tag your papers, and embed various forms of media to your notes.

Create New Paper

Manage

Manage your papers using the dashboard to duplicate, delete, or make small changes to your papers.

Create

Create notes without the restrictions of a typical word document. Write text, embed pictures and videos, and have access to them all online.

About

Team8Paper is part of a semester long Spring 2014 Georgia Tech CS4911 Senior Design Capstone project.
